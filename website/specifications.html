Understand Everything!

This new headset uses innovativ new technology to translate up to 50 different languages into english seemlessly. It uses the newest language recognition software from Google, and can translate it with almost no delay.


Easy Carrying-Case

This new specially designed carrying case is simple to use. It can easily be placed on a wireless charger to replenish the battery. Whilst on the go the case can be used to charge the headset itself, up to 5 more full charges. The headset has a batterylife of about 5h of continuous use.


No Wires in Sight

Now is truely the time of completely wireless headphones. And the translo headset is no exception! With a long lasting battery, wireless charging and new Bluetooth 5.1, your headset will truely be wireless, for a longer period.


Snug-Fit

The Headset is perfectly designed to fit in your ear with ease. There are extra nibbs, to make sure the headset can fit even into the most thiccest ears. They are also perfectly designed not to fall out of your ear whilst doing sports!


Gestures

One Tap

Tap the headphone once, to pause your music, or to accept a phonecall.

Long Press

Long Press the Headphone to activate translate mode. Once this is activated, the headset will automatically translate into your preferred language which can be selected in the app.

Swipe Up/Down

Swipe up or down to change volume.

Double Tap

Double tap the Headphone to skip a track, or decline a phonecall.
